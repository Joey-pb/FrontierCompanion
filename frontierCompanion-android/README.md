# Frontier Companion (Android) User Guide

---
Welcome to **Frontier Companion**, your digital guide to exploring the Frontier Culture Museum of Virginia. This application is designed to help you navigate outdoor exhibits, learn more about the history of the Old World and immigration to the frontiers of America, and find your way around the museum.

## Getting Started

---

When you first launch Frontier Companion, you will be greeted by the **Explore** screen. Use the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen to switch between the three main sections of the app:

- **Home**: General museum information and updates.
- **Search**: Find specific exhibit information or historical narratives.
- **Explore**: Explore the museum grounds interactively.

## Home Screen (Currently Under Development)

---

The **Home** screen is your hub for:

- Up-to-date information about museum events.
- Notices and general museum information.
- Visitor resources to help you plan your visit.

## Exploring the Map

---

The **Explore** section provides an interactive view of the museum.

- **Locating Exhibits**: Red markers represent different exhibits across the grounds.
- **Your Location**: If you grant location permissions, when at the museum, you can see your current position on the map, making it easy to find nearby sites.
- **Quick View**: Tap on any red marker to see a summary of the exhibit in a bottom sheet. This includes:
  - The exhibit's name.
  - A brief description.
  - A preview image.
  - A [View Exhibit](#viewing-exhibit-details) button for more details.
  - A [Create Route](#creating-routes) button (future release) to find your way there.

## Search Exhibit Narratives

---

Looking for something specific? Use **AI-powered** semantic search in the **Search** tab:

1.  Search by keyword, phrase, or topic.
2.  Press the search icon or enter on your keyboard.
3.  Tap on any result to view more details.

## Viewing Exhibit Details

---

When you select an exhibit from the Explore map, you can dive deep into its history:

- **Guided Panels**: Swipe through educational panels that tell the story of the exhibit..
- **Navigation Actions**:
  - **Locate on Map**: Instantly switch back to the map view centered on this exhibit.

## Creating Routes (Future Release)

---

Never get lost on the frontier!

1.  On the **Map**, tap a marker to open the exhibit summary.
2.  Tap the **"Create Route"** button.
3.  The app will highlight the destination and guide you from your current location.
    - _Ensure location services are enabled on your device for the best experience._
    - _The current implementation of the 'Create Route' button does not draw a route on the map. Temporary functionality zooms the map to the site location and highlights the map marker._
